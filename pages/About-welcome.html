Welcome Note

Greetings!

Thank you for visiting our SVA CE website for information.

Perhaps, you may be our prospective student, parent of a student, educationist, researcher or a citizen of the world. I hope that this site will provide all the College information that you are looking for.

Well, the College started with a humble beginning,years ago, with just 3 undergraduate programmes and has gradually grown up into a mammoth institution with 5 undergraduate programmes, 10 postgraduate programmes and research programmes. More information on the growth of the College over the years and details of the current programmes can be seen in the "About" web pages in this site. The members of the faculty and the facilities in the College are superb.

The Vision of the College is to develop the institution into a "CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE" offering engineering education and research. In our College, students not only earn their degrees in various disciplines but also learn to acquire total personality, emphasizing ethical values and preparing them to meet the growing challenges of the industry and diverse societal needs of our nation.

The college has shown great progress in various engineering and technology researches. Faculty members and students have been involved in many research areas. External funding agencies such as AICTE, DRDO, DST, and TNSCST have sponsored some of our research projects. The facilities for the extracurricular activities are ample and modern.
