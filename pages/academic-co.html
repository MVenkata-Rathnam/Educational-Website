Co-curricular Activities

The college is promoting its students’ innovative and leadership qualities by encouraging them to start associations in all departments so as to vent their ideas and to exchange their knowledge with other college students. To mention a few, the Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering branch has initiated ISOI Students’ chapter and a variety of academic activities are carried out under the auspices of this chapter. Through ISTE Students’ chapter, Electrical and Electronics Engineering branch organized various seminars and workshops for the benefit of the students.

Besides, the Electrical and Electronics budding engineers reverberates with their mirth in ‘Resonance’. MCA Department, through its ‘CLIQUE’ Association organized a symposium, seminars, workshops, in order to provide excellent opportunities to students and train them in various academic activities. The Computer Science branch conduct their programmes under their ‘CS ADROIT’. They have also conducted a National level conference SOFTCOM. The Electronics and Communication branch students are sharing their thoughts under the auspices of ‘NEOPOLES’. The Information Technology students communicate their messages through ‘XPLOITS’. The Science Departments, to their credit have a few finer moments with their ‘Sir C. V. Raman Science Association’. The English and the Mathematics departments also present their passion through ‘CONFLUENCE’ and ‘Euler.Com’ respectively.

The college constantly encourages the students to participate in all intercollegiate activities such as seminars, symposiums, workshops, cultural as well as sports activities.

Social Service

Our Rotract Club, which is actively organizing and participating in a number of programmes, won ‘The Best College Based Club’ award, and ‘The Best Community Based Project’ award, Our NSS unit is known for its constant activities round the year.
